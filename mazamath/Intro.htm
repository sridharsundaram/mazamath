Bridge to Lanka

Ravana, the ten-headed demon king, has kidnapped the damsel Sita and taken her across the ocean to his kingdom - Lanka.

You have to build a bridge across the sea to Lanka to rescue Sita.

GCD - If you see a key and treasure, you have to decide how much to jump so that you can collect both key and treasure.

LCM - If you and your shadow are separated, both of you have to meet at the same point. You and your shadow may have different jump lengths.

How to play

  • The Jump length can be changed only at the start position and only for GCD. Drag player using the right mouse button to change.
  • Pull (Drag) the player using the left mouse button to shoot off forwards or backwards for some number of jumps.
  • Scroll by dragging the screen with the mouse
  • LCM and GCD problems alternate for now.

BEWARE: Ravana's sea dragon Sinhika patrols the sea on the lookout for rescuers.
