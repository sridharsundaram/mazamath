Bridge to Lanka

How to play

  • The Jump length can be changed only at the start position and only for GCD. Drag player using the right mouse button to change.
  • Pull (Drag) the player or its shadow using the left mouse button to shoot off forwards or backwards for some number of jumps.
  • Scroll by dragging the screen with the mouse
  • LCM and GCD problems alternate for now.
  • GCD problems have key and treasure
  • LCM problems have player and shadow with different jumps.

BEWARE: Once you start, stay away from Sinhika.
Each time she catches you, she inflicts damage.
