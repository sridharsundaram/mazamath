Bridge to Lanka

Click on your icon or on your shadow icon to jump. You can jump only in multiples of jump strength. Click left mouse and drag to increase the multiple or change direction of jump.

Click right mouse and drag to change jump strength. This is allowed only when you are at the starting location for the key and treasure game.

BEWARE: Once you start, stay away from Sinhika.
Each time she catches you, she inflicts damage.
