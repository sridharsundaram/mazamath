Bridge to Lanka

Ravana, the ten-headed demon king, has kidnapped the damsel Sita and taken her across the ocean to his kingdom - Lanka.

You and have to build a bridge across the sea to Lanka to rescue Sita.

If you see a key and treasure, you have to decide how much to jump so that you can collect both key and treasure. Jump can be changed only when you are standing at the starting point.

If you and your shadow are separated, both of you have to meet but each of you have different jump strengths

BEWARE: Ravana's sea dragon Sinhika patrols the sea on the lookout for rescuers.
